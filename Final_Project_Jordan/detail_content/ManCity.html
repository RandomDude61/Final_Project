Man City 2023/24

Description

From 1967 to 1971, no team could match the style and confidence of Manchester City F.C.. Their end-to-end, pass-and-move style of attacking football was truly ahead of its time. So, as they won every domestic title and topped it off with a European Cup Winners' Cup, they did so in a way that, half a century later, is inspiring a new generation of Cityzens as they continue to push the boundaries at home and in Europe. This season, Manchester City F.C. and PUMA pay tribute to the brave team of entertainers and their leader: club icon Colin Bell. Manchester City F.C. 2022/23 fixtures Home features a classic design of the era, placing the club crest in the center and burgundy trim on the cuffs. The crown logo inside the neckline pays homage to "Colin the King".
