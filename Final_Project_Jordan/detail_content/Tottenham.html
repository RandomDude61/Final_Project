The recycled polyester used in Nike products begins as recycled plastic bottles, which are cleaned, shredded into flakes and converted into pellets. From there, the pellets are spun into new, high-quality yarn used in our products, delivering peak performance with a lower impact on the environment. In addition to reducing waste, recycled polyester produces up to 30% lower carbon emissions than virgin polyester.
