Description

Classic Azzurri. The 2024 Italy Home jersey celebrates a legacy of blue shirts and brilliant football. Featuring gold accents, retro fonts, and proud references to the triumphs of 1934, 1938, 1982, and 2006, the front of the jersey is split into tonal quadrants to honour the four generations of World Champions.
